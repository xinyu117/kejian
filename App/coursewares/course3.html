Lesson 1: Advanced Grammar Structures

In this lesson, we'll explore complex grammatical structures that will help you express ideas more precisely and eloquently.

1.1 Subjunctive Mood (虚拟语气)

The subjunctive mood is used to express hypothetical situations, wishes, or conditions contrary to reality.

Grammar Pattern

If + Past Simple, would + base form

  • If I had more time, I would learn Chinese.
  • If she were here, she would help us.

Key Vocabulary

  • hypothetical /ˌhaɪpəˈθetɪkl/ - 假设的
  • eloquent /ˈeləkwənt/ - 雄辩的，有说服力的
  • precisely /prɪˈsaɪsli/ - 精确地
  • contrary /ˈkɒntrəri/ - 相反的

Lesson 2: Business English Communication

Effective communication in business requires specific vocabulary and formal structures.

2.1 Formal Email Writing

Sample Business Email

Subject: Meeting Request - Project Discussion


Dear Mr. Johnson,

I hope this email finds you well. I am writing to request a meeting to discuss the upcoming marketing project.

Would you be available for a meeting next Tuesday at 2:00 PM? The meeting should take approximately one hour.

Please let me know if this time works for you, or suggest an alternative that would be more convenient.

Thank you for your time and consideration.

Best regards,
Sarah Chen
Marketing Manager

Business Phrases

  • I hope this email finds you well - 希望您一切安好
  • I am writing to... - 我写信是为了...
  • Would you be available...? - 您是否有空...?
  • Thank you for your consideration - 感谢您的考虑

Lesson 3: Conversation Practice

3.1 Job Interview Dialogue

Sample Interview

Interviewer: Tell me about yourself and your background.

Candidate: Well, I have a bachelor's degree in Computer Science and three years of experience in software development. I'm particularly interested in web technologies and have worked on several successful projects.

Interviewer: What are your strengths and weaknesses?

Candidate: My strength is my attention to detail and ability to work well under pressure. As for weaknesses, I sometimes spend too much time perfecting my work, but I'm learning to balance quality with efficiency.

Interview Vocabulary

  • background - 背景，经历
  • particularly - 特别地
  • attention to detail - 注重细节
  • under pressure - 在压力下
  • efficiency - 效率
